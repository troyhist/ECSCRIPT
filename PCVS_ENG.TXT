                   PcvsView 2.32 Universal Graphics Viewer!

Syntax of program:

     PCVSVIEW /?         - to get given reference
     PCVSVIEW <filename> - starting a view program depending
                           on extensions of filename

Processed extensions:

     PAL          - palettes of format PAL96C
     CGA, VGA     - dumps of memory of video modes CGA and VGA
     F16, F14, F8 - fonts of standard BitFont (Evafont)
     LMO          - sprites of format LMO96C
     SPR          - libraries of sprites of format SPR98a
     GIF          - pictures of format GIF87a
     TXC          - text files with macro commands of library
